As parents, we always try to find the best toys, best books, and best schools for our kids. However, when come to summer camps, day care centers, most of information is scattered, localized, isolated, and biased. Our mission is to build a social website such that parents can aggregate information about kids care facilities, compare and rate them, share the information with other parents.
